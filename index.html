My Travel Sites
  1. Sanibel Island, Florida
    2. Cool Places To Go In Sanibel Pictures
    Tarpon Bay, which is a home to mangrove
    trees and lots of unique animals. You can go on
    kayak tours or fishing tours there.
    J.N. Ding Darling Wildlife Refuge, which
    has lots of unique creatures and crocodiles.
    It's great for nature tours and biking.
    Sanibel Lighthouse, which is a lighthouse
    that has been around since 1884. It also
    has a great beach and late at night the
    beach is crawling with unique seashells.

  2. Ireland

    3. Cool Places To Go In Ireland Pictures
    Titanic Museum in Belfast, which is a
    history of how the Titanic was built, shows
    what the rooms in the Titanic were like, and
    what happened after the ship sank.
    Giants Causeway, which is a natural rock
    formation that has developed over millions
    of years.
    Newgrange, which is basically a giant
    cemetery that lights up only on the winter
    solstice.
  3. Iceland
    4. Cool Places To Go In Iceland Pictures
    The Blue Lagoon, which is a spa that was
    built on a natural hot spring. There are lots
    of good minerals in the water and you can
    put on a mud mask which is supposed to
    make your skin feel smoother.
    The Dynjandi Waterfall, which is a really
    giant waterfall that slides down the edge of
    a cliff.
